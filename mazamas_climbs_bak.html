Mazamas Climbing

All our climbs are open to members and non-members.

We have climbs for all abilities — from beginner through advanced levels.

You do not need to take any of our classes as prerequisites for a climb.

The Mazamas are not a guiding service.  If you want to climb and have no experience, see the Oregon guiding companies link for more info on professional guiding services.

Mountaineering

Welcome to Mazamas climbing! 

Summer Climb Schedule

Posted to the web April 1. The summer climb season is May 1 to Nov. 30. The online climb schedule is dynamic and will update when leaders add, cancel, or update the status of their climbs. Check back frequently! To get more details and full policies for summer climbing, go here. Mail your climb cards to the climb leader:

Winter Climb Schedule

The winter climb season is Dec 1 to April 30. Schedule is constantly updated as leaders add climbs. Check back frequently. Mail your climb cards to the climb leader.

Getting Started

If you’re new to climbing with the Mazamas, check the Getting Started page first.  It’s got an overview of the application process, top tips for getting on climbs, suggested beginner routes, an example of how to fill out a climb application card, and more.

Hike to the Summit

Want to stand on top of a Cascade Volcano, but don’t have any experience?  No problem! Check out the Hike to the Summit Program

Route Descriptions

One of the main features of our site is a ‘mini-guidebook” to over 120 routes we climb.  While access to this is through the Member pages, here’s a sample route description to whet your appetite.  (Access to ALL the route descriptions is one more reason to join the Mazamas!)

Climbing Mt. Hood FAQ

Climbing Mt. Hood is a lifelong dream for many.  We want to help!  If you plan to climb with us, with a guide or with friends, read our climbing Mt. Hood FAQ page to get started.

Learning the Ropes

Want to learn more climbing skills?  We offer beginning through advanced classes in rock climbing, mountaineering, crevasse rescue, rock anchors, ski mountaineering and more.  See the classes and education pages for details.

Oregon Guides

If you have needs beyond those of our classes, then learn skills, climb Mt. Hood (and more) with these guiding companies.

Climb Leadership

All our climbs are led by dedicated volunteers, who go through a comprehensive training program.  We’d be glad to consider you.  More on the Climb Leader development program
